Healthcare System Under Pressure

Many hospitals are facing capacity issues as COVID-19 cases rise again. Governments are increasing ICU beds and ensuring better preparedness.

  • ICU Beds: 20% more beds added globally.
  • Shortage of Staff: Many hospitals are recruiting more healthcare workers.
  • Government Support: Funding increased for hospital infrastructure.
